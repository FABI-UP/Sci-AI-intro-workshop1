A one-day, hands-on AI training workpack for biologists and postgraduate researchers, prepared by the **Forestry & Agricultural Biotechnology Institute (FABI), University of Pretoria**.

## Design

A single, offline-first design system built around **FABI green** — no external dependencies, theme-aware (light/dark), and print-friendly.

## The four ideas that outlast any tool

1. **Context is everything** — put the right things on the desk.
2. **Role · Task · Context · Format** — the anatomy of a good prompt.
3. **Climb the ladder** — Chat → Projects → Cowork → Agents.
4. **Verify against the source** — every time, whether a human or an AI produced it.
